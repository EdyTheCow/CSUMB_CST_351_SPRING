Message from the Executive Director


Welcome, and thank you for your interest in Gathering for Women.

Since our day center opened in June 2018, our staff and volunteers have served 520 individuals identifying as women with nearly 18,000 nutritious meals, and provided over 4,500 visits to our clothes closet and 2,600 showers. More than half of the guests we serve are over 50, with the eldest being 92. Day center services include: case management, breakfast/lunch meal service, showers, laundry vouchers, clothes closet, job readiness training, enrichment classes, and referrals. Please scroll down for more information about these services and the various ways you can support our efforts to deliver them to the homeless and housing-insecure women on the Monterey Peninsula.

Please visit my page for more about current activities at Gathering for Women. Thank you for your support!
