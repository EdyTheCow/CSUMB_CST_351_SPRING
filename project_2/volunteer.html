Volunteer Opportunities

Our volunteers are the heart of Gathering for Women. We could not do what we do without their gift of time and talent. We would love you to join our team!

The following is a list of all of the volunteer opportunities currently available (click on title for more information):

  • Front Desk Volunteer
  • Building A Program Host
  • Building B Program Host
  • Next Step Volunteer Case Manager/Navigator
  • Breakfast/Service Volunteer
  • Lunch Prep/Service/Clean Up Volunteer
  • Clothes Closet/Personal Care (CC/PC) Volunteer
  • Clothes Closet/Personal Care (CC/PC) Donation Drop-Off Volunteer
  • Garden Volunteer

Shifts run from 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Monday through Thursday and 8:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m. on Fridays. Weekday breakfast service starts at 7:15 a.m. We prefer two hour increments - however we welcome any time commitment!

To volunteer, contact Betty Nebb at betty@gatheringforwomen.org.
